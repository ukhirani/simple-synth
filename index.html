Powerful Features

Oscillator

Multiple waveform options including sine, square, sawtooth, and triangle. Adjust octave, semitone, and noise levels for precise sound shaping.

ADSR Envelope

Full ADSR (Attack, Decay, Sustain, Release) controls for precise sound shaping and dynamic response.

Filter

Adjustable frequency and resonance controls to sculpt your sound with precision.

Chorus

Add depth and movement to your sounds with rich chorus effects.

Reverb

Create spacious, atmospheric sounds with adjustable reverb parameters.

Crunch

Add warmth and character with analog-style saturation and distortion.

Limiter

Prevent clipping and control dynamics with the built-in limiter.
